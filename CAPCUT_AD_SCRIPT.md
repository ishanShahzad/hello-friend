# 🎬 CapCut AI Video Ad Script for Rozare Platform

## 📋 CapCut AI Prompt

```
Create a professional video advertisement using the provided studio/podcast image. The man in the image should speak directly to the camera with natural, engaging expressions and gestures. His facial expressions should match the emotion of each sentence - concerned when discussing problems, excited when presenting solutions, and confident when describing features. Use smooth transitions between topics with appropriate hand gestures for emphasis. The tone should be conversational, friendly, and professional - like a trusted advisor speaking to a friend. Add subtle background music that builds excitement. The pacing should be dynamic with slight pauses for emphasis on key benefits.
```

## 🎨 Visual Direction for CapCut AI

### Expression Guide:
- **Opening (0-20s):** Concerned, empathetic, understanding - slight frown, nodding
- **Solution (20-30s):** Excited, eyes widening, leaning forward
- **Features (30-75s):** Confident, smiling, using hand gestures for emphasis
- **Closing (75-90s):** Warm, inviting, direct eye contact, reassuring smile

### Gesture Suggestions:
- **"FREE"** - Emphasize with open hands gesture
- **"AI assistant"** - Point to head/temple (intelligence)
- **"WhatsApp"** - Gesture like holding phone
- **"Custom subdomain"** - Hands spreading apart (expansion/growth)
- **"Join now"** - Welcoming gesture with open arms

### Pacing:
- Slow down on key benefits (15-day free trial, AI, custom subdomain)
- Speed up slightly on problem statements (creates urgency)
- Pause for 1 second after major benefits
- End with confident, slower delivery

## 🎬 CapCut AI Settings Recommendations

### Video Settings:
- **Aspect Ratio:** 16:9 (YouTube, website) or 9:16 (Instagram, TikTok)
- **Duration:** 60-90 seconds (full) or 30-45 seconds (short)
- **Voice Tone:** Professional, friendly, conversational
- **Speaking Speed:** Medium (not too fast, not too slow)
- **Background:** Keep original studio/podcast setting

### Audio:
- **Background Music:** Upbeat, modern, tech-inspired (low volume)
- **Voice Volume:** Clear and prominent over music
- **Sound Effects:** Subtle "ding" or "whoosh" on key benefits

### Text Overlays (Optional):
- "FREE" - when mentioned
- "AI-Powered" - when discussing AI features
- "Rozare.com" - at the end
- Key benefits as bullet points

### Transitions:
- Smooth cuts between topics
- Slight zoom-in on important points
- Fade to black at end with website URL
